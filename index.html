📌 When is the orientation for Humanities Students at NMU?

There's no specific date of which you are required to attend and not attend. All there is to know is that you should attend from the 3rd of February to the 7th of February at South Campus. For more details, download the NMU Humanities Orientation timetable here.

🔑Hi, does the nsfas have a budget for loan? Coz all statuses for students loan is loan in progress

Yes, NSFAS has a budget for student loans in 2025. These loans are for students with a household income between R350,001 and R600,000. Eligibility is based on financial need and academic performance. The "Loan in Progress" status means your application is under review. NSFAS is verifying your financial and academic details before approval. Ensure all required documents are submitted to avoid delays. Over 17,000 loan applications have been received, so processing may take time. Check your NSFAS portal regularly for updates. For more info: https://www.nsfas.org.za/content/.

🔑 What is the link for change of course nmu

To change your course at Nelson Mandela University (NMU), you'll need to complete the "Change of Qualification Application Form." You can download this form directly from NMU's website: After completing the form, submit it to your Faculty Administrative Officer for further processing.
